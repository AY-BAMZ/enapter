Generation Hydrogen.
Spring 2021

We organize the Generation Hydrogen program, an event centered around the theme of trailblazing hydrogen technology. It brings together students, experts and all those fighting climate change with green hydrogen. A place to show the world how hydrogen innovation will impact our lives.

GrabCAD Challenges

Enapter is hosting Challenges to design devices for hydrogen applications. Anyone can participate by submitting their ideas via the GrabCAD platform. Winners will not only receive prizes, but also get the chance to develop their ideas with the Enapter team.
